# Ramsar Year

## Current guidance

If public copy needs the Ramsar year, use the designation date from the official Ramsar Sites Information Service entry: `2019-02-02`.

If the year is not important to the sentence, prefer `Ramsar Site No. 2419`.

## Why the repo had a mismatch

- The RSIS entry for `Complejo Los Cobanos` shows `Date of Designation: 2019-02-02`.
- Ramsar materials also show later `2020` publication or announcement dates, which created the earlier ambiguity.

## Source
