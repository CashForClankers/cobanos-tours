# Wreck Names

## Current safe public wording

Keep public copy to `Douglas` and `Cheribon` unless a stronger source trail resolves the name mismatch.

## Why

- El Salvador Divers lists `SS Douglas` and `SS Cheribon`.
- Visit El Salvador adds `SS Arce`.
- La Prensa Gráfica reports three documented historic wrecks in the area: `Columbus`, `Cheribon`, and `Douglas`.

That is enough to say Los Cóbanos has documented historic wrecks, but not enough to publish four distinct names as settled fact.

## Working rule

- `Douglas` and `Cheribon` are safe named examples.
- `Columbus` and `Arce` stay in internal notes until the mismatch is resolved.
