The Natural Wonder

El Salvador's Hidden Underwater World

Los Cóbanos is home to the only significant coral reef ecosystem in El Salvador — and one of the most biodiverse marine protected areas on the entire Pacific coast of Central America.

The reef sits on ancient volcanic rock formations, creating a labyrinth of caverns, arches, and coral gardens teeming with life. The area is a designated Área Natural Protegida managed in partnership with local fishing communities.

About Us

Local Guides, Real Knowledge

We are a team of local fishermen, marine conservationists, and coastal community members who have called Los Cóbanos home for generations. We started guiding tours to share this extraordinary place — and to fund its protection.

Every tour contributes directly to the Tortuguero cooperative and local reef conservation efforts. When you book with us, you're supporting the community that protects this reef year-round.
