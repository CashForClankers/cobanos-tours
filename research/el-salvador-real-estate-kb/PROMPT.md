Expand this knowledge base with disciplined research.

## Objective

Turn a seed investor article into a durable El Salvador real-estate research corpus that separates:
- sourced fact
- inference
- unresolved questions

## Rules

1. Prefer primary sources first:
- IMF
- World Bank
- BCR
- CEPAL
- official Salvadoran ministries, registries, or regulators

2. Search Spanish first when the topic is:
- tourism statistics
- property law
- land registry or transaction mechanics
- municipal permitting
- local planning
- housing policy

3. Do not flatten the market into one story.
Break findings into:
- national macro
- submarket behavior
- tourism corridors
- diaspora / remittance demand
- legal and financing mechanics

4. Every wiki page should use:
- `Sourced`
- `Inference`
- `Needs verification`

5. Keep language concise and analytical.
Avoid hype.
