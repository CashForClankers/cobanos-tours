# Risks

## Macro and policy

`Sourced`
- IMF documents still emphasize fiscal consolidation, governance reform, and Bitcoin-risk mitigation.
- World Bank overview still flags climate resilience, productivity, and job quality as unresolved issues.

`Inference`
- This is still a market where narrative can outrun capacity.
- A strong tourism story does not remove sovereign, fiscal, or governance risk.

## Market-structure risk

`Needs verification`

## Narrative risk

`Inference`
- A lot of commentary treats national reputation change as if it automatically converts into deep real-estate demand.
- That may be true only in a handful of neighborhoods, corridors, or product types.
